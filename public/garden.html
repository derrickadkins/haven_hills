Rent a Garden

Welcome to Heaven Hill Farm! We offer garden rentals for individuals and families who want to grow their own fruits and vegetables. Our gardens are located on a sunny hillside with rich, fertile soil that is perfect for growing a wide variety of crops. Each garden plot is 10 feet by 10 feet and comes with access to water and gardening tools. You can rent a garden for a full year for $100, which includes tilling and preparation of the soil. We also offer optional services such as planting, weeding, and harvesting for an additional fee. Whether you are an experienced gardener or a beginner, our staff will help you get started and provide guidance throughout the growing season. Rent a garden today and enjoy the satisfaction of growing your own fresh, organic produce!

Cost: $100 per year

Optional Services:
Planting: $25
Weeding: $15
Harvesting: $20
